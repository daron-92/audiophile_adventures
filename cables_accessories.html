Cables&Accessories

Neotech KS-1034M

The Neotech KS-1034M loudspeaker cable was my first foray into serious hi-fi accessories, marking a significant step in my audio journey. It occupied a unique price point—not outrageously expensive, but still substantial enough to cost half as much as my amplifier. At the time, this felt like a considerable investment, and I was quite proud to have it as part of my system. However, to be honest, I didn't notice much of a difference in sound quality after upgrading to this cable. This isn’t to say I’m skeptical about the impact of high-quality cables—quite the opposite. I firmly believe that well-made cables can influence sound quality, but I’ve come to realize that it requires a certain level of system refinement to truly appreciate these differences. Looking back, the Neotech KS-1034M was more of a symbolic purchase for me, representing my growing commitment to high-fidelity audio. It taught me an important lesson: while cables do matter, their impact becomes more noticeable as the rest of the system reaches higher levels of performance. So while this cable may not have revolutionized my listening experience at the time, it was a crucial step in my ongoing pursuit of better sound.
